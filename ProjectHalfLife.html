Project Half-Life

Project Description

A Virtual Hackathon that aims to connect the smartest minds all around the world to collaborate and build something that will have a true and lasting impact. A hackathon where participants come up with a solution to a real-world problem, prototype their solution, and present to a panel of judges, all in 48 hours.

I started this project during quarantine at the end of junior year. Noticed that many virtual hackathons lacked a sense of community and failed to bring enough of the hacker experience to participants. I, along with a friend, tackled this problem by introducing the unique solution of food delivery, through the use of various APIs, to participants; a solution never heard of before in virtual hackathons. We are currently working out the logistics and have plans to contact sponsors for funding soon, with the expected event date to be in the spring of 2021.
