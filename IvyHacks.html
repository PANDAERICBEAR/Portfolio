IvyHacks

Project Date October 2nd, 2020
Role Hacker, Participant

IvyHacks - Having been full-remote for almost a month in October 2020, I had a pretty good grasp of the problems with full-remote online learning. I noticed that there was a huge gap in communication between the teacher, fully-online students, and in-person students. I wanted to develop a solution to bridge this lack of communication and community. I developed a web-based VR classroom-environment using a lightweight javascript game engine to try and bring the community back to what it was before Covid-19.

Imported a classroom-environment to make the surroundings less foreign. Included multiplayer to bring classmates and teachers into the space to imitate a typical in-person classroom, through the use of a database. Incorporated options to bring the class to virtual field trips anywhere in the world like The Louvre or the Eiffel Tower. Also allowed for options to play a 3D video as the classroom environment. Imagine sitting in class while the civil war was happening in the background. How cool is that!!?
